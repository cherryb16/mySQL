# Extra Credit Assignment: Verify an Airline 10-K Claim Using SQL

Choose an airline (e.g., Southwest, JetBlue, American, Delta, etc.) and skim through one of its 10-K filings. You can easily find these by Googling something like:

> "American Airlines 2019 10-K"

Within the 10-K, find a **fact-based statement** that you believe could be verified using the **Domestic Market table** in the Allegiant database.

**Example:** An airline might list its main hubs or largest airports. Presumably, hubs correspond to the airports where the airline has the most traffic. In that case, your goal would be to write a SQL query that identifies the 10 largest airports served by that airline in 2019 using the Domestic Market data.

### Your Submission Should Include:

1. The airline and 10-K filing you used
2. The fact-based statement from the 10-K, copied and pasted as a **comment** in your SQL query
3. The SQL query you wrote to verify that statement

> It's completely fine if your results don't match the statement perfectly. If your results differ from the claim in the 10-K, briefly suggest a possible explanation for the discrepancy (e.g., differences in how hubs are defined, time period differences, domestic vs. total traffic, etc.).
